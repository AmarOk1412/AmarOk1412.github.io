Brussels

Beer, waffles and buildings

The day before the FOSDEM, I visited Brussels. It was a 26 km day walk. In the morning I went to Atomium built for the World’s Fair Expo (1958). Then I wanted to go to the “Serres Royales”, but it was raining so I come back in the center.

In the center I saw the Palace of Justice a great building you can see far away. In this area, you have a lot of buildings. For example, you can see la Pharmacie anglaise, the palace of Brussels, etc.

At 3:00 I ate near the Galeries Saint-Hubert and I visited the Grand Place (UNESCO World Heritage Site) where you can find the guild houses and I finished to visit the area by seeing the Manneken Pis and a graffiti of Tintin.
